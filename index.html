About me

Hello, my name is FanFan, who is keen to explore the whole new world. My major was computer science and technology during my undergraduate courses, with the result that I developed the basic skills of programming and databases, such as C, C++, SQL. After graduating from China, I started to take a close interest in design, particularly in user experience. To have further development, I applied to Monash Univerity which is a top-ranked school to learn interaction design in 2017.


After a two-year hard working in Australia, I graduated with distinction in July 2019 and will get the certificate of Master's Degree in October. During my university life in Australia, I volunteered on the campus, receiving a Certificate of Participation from Monash Postgraduate Association and Certificate of Accreditation from Mental Health Aid Australia. Such experience enables me to have the ability to cooperate with people from a range of backgrounds. To be a good designer, cross-disciplinary knowledge, I believe, is the cornerstone for career development.
